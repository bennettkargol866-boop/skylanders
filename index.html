Controls

  • WASD or Arrows to move
  • Move the Mouse to aim your hero
  • Left Click to swing the basic attack
  • Right Click or E for the special ability
  • Space to jump
  • Find the key, then bring it to the gate
  • Enter to continue after clearing an area
  • R to restart after defeat
